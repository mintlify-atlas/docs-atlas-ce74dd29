<Note>
  You need valid building credentials (email and password) to log in. Contact your building administrator if you don't have an account.
</Note>

## Install the app

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Download APK">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download the latest release">
        Go to [github.com/nathan101000/openly/releases](https://github.com/nathan101000/openly/releases) and download the latest `.apk` file.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Allow installation from unknown sources">
        On your Android device, open **Settings** → **Apps** → **Special app access** → **Install unknown apps** and allow your browser or file manager to install APKs.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install the APK">
        Open the downloaded `.apk` file and tap **Install**. Android will prompt you to confirm.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Build from source">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install Flutter">
        Make sure you have the Flutter SDK installed with Dart SDK `>=3.0.0 <4.0.0`. See the [Flutter installation guide](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install) for your platform.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Clone the repository">
        ```bash
        git clone https://github.com/nathan101000/openly.git
        cd openly
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install dependencies">
        ```bash
        flutter pub get
        ```
      </Step>
      <Step title="Build the APK">
        ```bash
        flutter build apk
        ```
        The output APK is written to `build/app/outputs/flutter-apk/app-release.apk`. Transfer it to your device and install it.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Log in for the first time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch Openly">
    Open the app. You'll see the animated splash screen — a fingerprint scan sequence that transitions to an unlocked padlock — for about 4 seconds before the app loads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    On the **Door Access Login** screen, enter your **Email** and **Password**, then tap **Login**.

    If login succeeds, Openly stores your session token and takes you straight to the home screen on future launches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up biometrics (optional)">
    After your first successful password login, Openly detects whether your device supports biometric authentication. On subsequent launches, a fingerprint icon appears on the login screen — tap it to authenticate without typing your password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Unlock a door

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the door list">
    The home screen shows all available doors organized by floor. Use the **Search** bar at the top to filter by door name, or switch to the **Favorites** tab for doors you've saved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Unlock">
    Tap the **Unlock** button on any door card. Openly immediately sends a pulse unlock signal — no extra confirmation step needed. A 5-second countdown appears at the bottom of the screen while the door is open.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Timed unlock (optional)">
    Need the door open for longer? Long-press the **Unlock** button to choose between a 5-second pulse or a timed unlock (1–60 minutes), then confirm your choice.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Add frequently used doors to your favorites by tapping the star icon on any door card. They'll appear in the **Favorites** tab for one-tap access.
</Tip>
