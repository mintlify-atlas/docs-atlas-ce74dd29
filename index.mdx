## What Openly does

Openly connects to your building's door access API and lets you:

- **Browse doors** organized by floor, with smart categorization for suites, washrooms, staircases, and conference rooms
- **Unlock doors** with a single tap — choose a timed unlock (minutes) or a pulse
- **Save favorites** for the doors you use most, surfaced in a dedicated tab
- **Search and filter** the full door list instantly
- **Authenticate securely** using your username and password, with optional biometric (fingerprint) login
- **Update automatically** via over-the-air updates delivered through GitHub Releases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Download the latest APK from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/nathan101000/openly/releases) or build from source using `flutter build apk`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Enter your building access credentials (email and password). Openly stores your session securely and prompts you to re-authenticate only when your token expires.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your door">
    Browse the door list by floor, use the search bar to filter by name, or jump to your saved favorites.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock">
    Tap a door card and confirm the unlock. The door opens for the configured duration (default: 5 seconds). You'll see a confirmation animation when the unlock succeeds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
