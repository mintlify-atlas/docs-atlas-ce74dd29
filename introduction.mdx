Openly gives you instant, secure access to the doors in your building — right from your Android device. Built with Flutter and Material 3, it connects to your building's door access API and lets you unlock doors with a single tap, save favorites, search by name, and stay up to date automatically.

The app's primary color is `#4b5c92`, customizable through the in-app color picker in **Settings**.

## Key features

- **Token-based authentication** — Log in with your email and password. Sessions persist across restarts; you are redirected to the login screen when your token expires.
- **Biometric login** — Authenticate with your fingerprint after your first password login, using `local_auth`.
- **Door control** — Unlock any door via pulse or timed unlock (default: 5 seconds). Animated feedback confirms every action.
- **Favorites tab** — Save your most-used doors for quick access in a dedicated tab.
- **Search and filter** — Instantly filter the full door list by name or floor.
- **Material 3 design** — Light and dark theme support with a seed-color-based palette you can customize.
- **OTA updates** — Openly checks for new releases on GitHub and installs updates in-app, with a changelog dialog before each update.
- **User profile and settings** — Manage your session, change your theme seed color, and view version info from **Settings**.
