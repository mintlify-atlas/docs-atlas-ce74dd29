## The door list

The **Doors** tab shows every door you have access to, organized by floor. Each floor has its own labeled section header. Scroll down to browse all available doors across the building.

At the top of the screen, a persistent **Search** bar lets you filter doors by name as you type. Below the search bar, a row of filter chips lets you narrow the list to a specific floor — tap **All** to show every door again.

### Door types

Every door in the list belongs to one of the following categories:

| Type | Examples |
|---|---|
| Suite | Office suites identified by room number (e.g., Suite 604) |
| Men's washroom | Men's restrooms on a given floor |
| Women's washroom | Women's restrooms on a given floor |
| Staircase | Stairwell access doors |
| Conference | Conference room doors |
| Floor door | Other floor-level doors not in the categories above |
| Building-level | Lobby, café, main entrance, parking, and other common areas not tied to a specific floor |

### Building-level vs. floor-specific doors

Doors that belong to a specific floor (suites, washrooms, stairwells, conference rooms) appear under their floor heading. Doors that serve the whole building — such as the lobby, café, or main entrance — are grouped under **Main Building** at the top of the list.

## Unlocking a door

Each door card shows an **Unlock** button. There are two ways to use it:

### Pulse unlock (tap)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the door">
    Scroll through the door list or use the search bar to locate the door you want to unlock. You can also use the floor filter chips to narrow the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Unlock">
    Tap the **Unlock** button on the door card. Openly immediately sends a pulse unlock signal to the door controller — no confirmation dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    On success, a 5-second countdown snackbar appears at the bottom of the screen (e.g., "Unlocked Suite 604"). The door releases briefly and re-locks automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Timed unlock (long press)

For situations where you need a door to stay open for a set period — such as during a move or a delivery — long-press the **Unlock** button to open the **Unlock Mode** sheet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press Unlock">
    Long-press the **Unlock** button on any door card. A bottom sheet appears with two options: **Pulse (5s)** and **Timed**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Timed">
    Tap **Timed** to reveal a duration slider. Drag the slider to select between 1 and 60 minutes, then tap **Timed** again to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Door stays open">
    The app sends the unlock command with your chosen duration. A snackbar confirms how long the door will stay open (e.g., "Unlocked Main Entrance for 5 min"). The door re-locks automatically after the time elapses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Add frequently used doors to your **Favorites** for one-tap access without searching. See [Favorites & Search](/features/favorites-and-search) for details.
</Tip>

## Refreshing the door list

If a door is missing or the list looks out of date, pull down from the top of the screen to refresh. Openly will re-fetch the full door list from the server and display a confirmation when the update is complete.
